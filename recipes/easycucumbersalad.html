Easy Cucumber Salad

Cucumber Salad is a light and refreshing side dish that is perfect for a summer night meal!

  • 2 english cucumbers
  • 1 red onion
  • ¼ cup rice wine vinegar
  • ¼ cup water
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp dried dill
  • 6 green onion
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp pepper
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp roasted sesame seeds
  1. Slice both cucumbers into thin round discs. Place in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Slice the onion into thin strips, then slice those strips in half moons. Place in the bowl with sliced cucumbers and add chopped green onions.
  3. In a separate small bowl combine the rice wine vinegar, water, sugar, dried dill, salt, pepper, sesame oil, minced garlic, and roasted sesame seeds. Mix well.
  4. Pour sauce over the cucumber and use tongs to thoroughly mix everything together.
  5. Refrigerate 30 minutes or until ready to serve.
